Hi there, I’m 5even. Over the past age I have worked full-time in various positions in the fields of publishing, advertising, and education, mainly responsible for the complete process of visual design from planning to execution. I have worked in a variety of fields, including visual identity (V.I.), print advertising, print publications, IT product interface UI\/UX, post-production photography, etc. But no matter which form of media is used, the core of it – “Visual Expression” – is always fascinating to me. Over the years, I’ve sought to find the most accurate and elegant ways to express a project’s character and strengths.
